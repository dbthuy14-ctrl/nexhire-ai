Our Mission

At NexHire AI, our mission is to eliminate subconscious bias from the hiring process. Traditional recruiting relies heavily on pedigree, zip codes, and university names. We think that's broken.

We built an artificial intelligence suite that evaluates individuals purely on their domain capabilities and logical reasoning, allowing forward-thinking companies to scale diverse, highly-skilled teams rapidly.

Why We Do It

Businesses spend billions annually on Headhunter fees, only to suffer terrible retention rates because candidates were hired for their charisma, rather than their competence.

Concurrently, brilliant minds face closed doors because their resume lacked a specific keyword parsed by ancient Applicant Tracking Systems. We are here to fix this dual-sided inefficiency through rigorous AI-driven evaluations.
