How It Works

From job posting to your next great hire in 4 simple steps.

01

AI Generates Job Post

Type a simple prompt (e.g., "Need a Senior React Dev"). Our built-in AI instantly writes an optimized, attractive job listing and defines the required skills.

02

Candidates Apply

Applicants submit their CV, add their LinkedIn profile, and upload a short 1-minute intro video to showcase their personality.

03

AI Evaluates & Ranks

Our AI screens CVs and administers automated tests (skills + logic + role-fit) to score and rank candidates objectively.

04

Review & Hire

You review the top-ranked shortlist visually on your dashboard and make the final hiring decision with confidence.
